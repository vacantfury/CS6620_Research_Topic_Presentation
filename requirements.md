This assignment will familiarize you with active research topics in cloud computing. A primary goal of this assignment will be to obtain a general picture of (reasonably) current research in cloud computing. A secondary goal will be to arrive at some specific topics that can be used to inform future research (in the form of independent study, capstone, or MSc thesis study) for students who are interested in pursuing these options. That said, the assignment is required of all students, whether or not they plan to pursue cloud computing research in the future.

For the assignment, each reading group will be assigned one of the following topic areas, along with a single research paper corresponding to this area. I have tried to select survey or overview research papers that touch upon multiple points of interest in each areas. I have also tried to select journal publications in IEEE or ACM journals in order to ensure a reasonable degree of academic legitimacy and impact. All students in the reading group should read the assigned paper and be prepared to discuss it.

In addition to the assigned paper, each student in the group should find one related academic paper. These should also be from reputable sources (IEEE Xplore and ACM Digital Library are generally good resources) but they may be short conference papers (journal papers are also okay) and they may be more narrow in their focus. Ideally, the selected research papers will fit under the general rubric of topics described by the assigned overview/survey paper that all students read.

Using these references (in addition to the assigned paper) students should produce a roughly 15 minute presentation describing the state of current research in the assigned topic. The presentation should also include at least one open research question that the presenting students believe would be worthy of future work. This research question may follow up on any of the student selected papers, but it should represent some future research that could be pursued in the field.


We will set aside class time for presentations. All students *must *present in person for full credit on this assignment. Students will upload their presentation slides as a PDF file here.

Each group's presentation should give an overview of the research area and also give a brief description of the work done in **each student's selected research paper. **Be sure that the presentation includes the titles, authors and publication information of each student's selected research paper.
